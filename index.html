FAQ

Questions HVAC owners usually ask first.

Which locations do you work with?+
Primarily the USA, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, and the UK — English-speaking markets where Google Search Ads and Local Services campaigns are well established for home-service businesses.
Do you only work with HVAC companies?+
HVAC is the primary focus. Plumbing, roofing, and electrical contractors are a close secondary fit, since the campaign structure, seasonality, and buyer intent are similar. Cleaning, landscaping, pest control, and garage door businesses are considered on a case-by-case basis.
What happens in the free Google Ads audit?+
A full review of your existing account (if you have one) or a starting plan (if you don't) — covering wasted spend, keyword match types, and whether your conversions, GTM, and GA4 are actually tracking correctly. You get a plain-English summary either way.
How much should I expect to spend on ads?+
It depends on your service area and how competitive your local market is. This gets discussed directly during the audit, once there's real account or market data to base a number on — not a number quoted before that.
Do I need a new website before running ads?+
Not necessarily. Your existing landing page gets reviewed first — sometimes a few targeted changes to speed, clarity, or the call-to-action are enough, rather than a full rebuild.
How is this different from a general PPC agency?+
The focus stays on Google Search Ads, Performance Max, Conversion Tracking, GTM, and GA4 for one industry — HVAC and adjacent home-service trades — rather than managing accounts across many unrelated industries at once.
